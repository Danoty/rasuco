Who We Are

A welcoming Adventist church rooted in Scripture and service.

Our identity

Hope Alive SDA Church Bondo is a local Seventh-day Adventist congregation dedicated to sharing the gospel, nurturing spiritual growth, and building a compassionate church family.

We are passionate about worship that honors God, Bible teaching that transforms lives, and mission that reaches people with practical love and eternal hope.

What defines us

  • Christ-centered worship
  • Biblical teaching
  • Sabbath fellowship
  • Prayerful community life
  • Compassionate outreach

Vision

To be a vibrant church where people encounter Jesus, grow in faith, and become faithful witnesses in their homes, community, and the world.

Mission

To proclaim the everlasting gospel, disciple believers through Scripture and fellowship, and serve humanity with compassion and integrity.
