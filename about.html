Mission

To help people know Jesus, understand Scripture, and live with hope.

Our mission is to create a prayerful, Bible-rooted, mission-minded church environment where children, youth, adults, and visitors can grow in faith, character, and service. We want every person who walks in to feel seen, welcomed, and spiritually nourished.

What that looks like in practice

  • Worship gatherings that are orderly, warm, and Christ-centered
  • Bible teaching that is understandable and spiritually grounded
  • Prayer rhythms that carry families, leaders, and members
  • Ministry spaces where people can belong and serve meaningfully

Vision

To be a vibrant church of faith, truth, compassion, and mission.

We envision a church that shines with biblical clarity, worship excellence, strong fellowship, and meaningful impact in Bondo and beyond.
