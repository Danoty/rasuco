What We Believe

Biblical faith centered on Jesus Christ.

The Bible

We believe Scripture is God's inspired Word and the sure foundation for faith, doctrine, and daily life.

Jesus Christ

We believe salvation is found in Jesus, whose life, death, resurrection, and ministry bring hope and redemption.

The Sabbath

We honor the seventh-day Sabbath as a sacred gift of worship, rest, fellowship, and renewal in God.

Grace & Salvation

We are saved by grace through faith, and transformed by the Holy Spirit into faithful disciples.

Second Coming

We live in hopeful expectation of Christ’s soon return and the restoration of all things.

Mission

We are called to share God’s love through witness, service, teaching, health, and compassionate ministry.
